Unified Analytics for Government

This will be an ongoing documentation of one method for implementing web analytics to track many government agency websites within a single Google Analytics profile, with the goal of populating a dashboarding tool such as analytics-reporter or city-analytics-dashboard.

This guide is under active development

About this Guide

I've included 2 ways to get up and running: The Easy Way, and the "I want to understand how to do this" way. In the near future, I'm planning to build a wizard-style version of The Easy Way (stay tuned).
